"What is an online examination?"

"What is an online examination? An online examination is a test that is administered over the internet. It allows participants to take the test remotely using a computer or mobile device with an internet connection.

Online examinations typically involve creating and delivering the exam through a secure online platform. Participants log in, answer questions within a specified time frame, and submit their responses electronically. The system can then automatically grade the answers or provide them to instructors for manual evaluation.
